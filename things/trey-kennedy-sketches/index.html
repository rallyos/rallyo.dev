Trey Kennedy Sketches

Thing of the week | Issue 2022-47 | 100 Words

It’s a tradition to mention comedy YouTube channels I follow, so here’s another one – Trey Kennedy.

He definitely has a unique niche in the comedy part of YouTube. He’s especially good when acting as a woman. His videos are very creative and funny.

My favourites are The 5 Phases of a Diet, The water jug skit at the beginning of this video, Bachelor vs. Bachelorette parties, Girls’ Night vs. Guys’ Night, Girl Problems vs. Guy Problems, and way more of them. Do check his channel, it’s lots of fun.
