MF Doom LoFi

Thing of the week | Issue 2022-26 | 100 Words

I’m a big fan of the hip-hop lo-fi remixes, but this week I got a surprise recommendation from YouTube – a lo-fi remix of MF Doom’s work.

Maybe it’s related to a search I did because right now, I’m in a bit of MF Doom phase, but either way the remix is pretty great. I recommend it strongly, especially for a night listen. More remixes from the creator can be found here.

Hip-hop was built on recycling great music, so I’m all hands up for such projects.
