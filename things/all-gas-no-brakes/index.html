All Gas No Brakes

Thing of the week | Issue 2021-18 | 100 Words

One of my favourite channels on YouTube. It’s hard to explain, so I guess a good starting point is this video although some may prefer jumping straight into some of their best work.

I would say it’s a channel documenting the American culture (mostly the bad parts, but hey…there’s people like this everywhere)

Once you watch one of them you can’t stop yourself from binging the whole thing.

I’m happy that someone is preserving what some people in late 2010s and early 2020s cared about. It could be a fun, side homework, for history study in the future.
