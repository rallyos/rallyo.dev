KillaKayTV's Youtube Channel

Thing of the week | Issue 2021-49 | 100 Words

Watching comedy on YouTube is one of my guilty pleasures. So, here comes another favourite of mine - KillaKayTV.

His sketches are one of the most original I’ve seen, because of his ability to portray every role in life – Mother, Salesmen, Barber, Walmart worker, Teacher, the list goes on. In most of his videos, he also shows a great talent of converting his house into a movie set.
