What Future

Thing of the week | Issue 2022-49 | 200 Words

My thing of the week this time is a new podcast from Joshua Topolsky (Engadget, The Verge, and other cool places on the internet). It’s called What Future.

I’ve been following him for many years, so I have no doubt that it will be very intriguing. So far, there were some really great episodes, although there is a bit too much Twitter talk currently (expected though, it’s kind of important).

So far, the episode with the Midjourney founder is my favourite, but the one with The Peripheral’s director is a second best.

Do follow it, I have no doubt that you will get a lot from it.
